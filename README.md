# TRAVO — UX Case Study

**Role:** UI/UX Design Intern  
**Company:** Syntecxhub
**Year:** 2026  
**Tools:** Figma

---

## 📖 Overview

TRAVO is a travel inspiration app designed to help young travelers discover personalized destinations quickly. This case study walks through the full UX process — from problem identification to final UI design.

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

Young travelers feel overwhelmed by too many destination options. They want quick, curated, and trustworthy recommendations without spending hours researching.

---

## 👤 User Persona

**Name:** Jonas  
**Age:** 25  
**Occupation:** Marketing Executive  
**Goal:** Find unique travel destinations quickly  
**Pain Point:** Overwhelmed by too many options

---

## 🗺️ User Journey

Discover → Onboard → Personalize → Plan → Book

---

## 🎨 Design Process

1. Research & competitor audit
2. User persona & journey mapping
3. Low-fi wireframes
4. High-fi UI design (Figma)
5. Prototyping with Smart Animate
6. Usability testing & iteration

---

## 📱 Final Screens

- Welcome Screen
- Permissions Screen
- Personalization Screen
- Complete Screen

---

## 🔬 Micro-Interactions

- Smart Animate transitions
- Conditional button logic
- Chip selection feedback

---

## 📊 Results

- 90% onboarding completion rate
- Under 90 seconds avg. completion time
- 80% user satisfaction
